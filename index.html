About

I'm Ernie Hsiung—developer, educator, and lifelong explainer of complicated things. My career has taken me from coding at startups and civic tech projects to teaching hundreds of new developers how to build their first web apps—and now, helping people understand and actually use AI without needing a PhD or a VC check.

I've built journaling tools powered by Large Language Models (LLMs), created custom GPT workflows, and trained everyone from individual entrepreneurs to nonprofit operations teams on practical AI implementation. I combine full-stack development skills with UX design intuition, plus extensive experience explaining complex concepts through digital communication and in-person collaboration.

People say I'm especially good at translating technical ideas for non-technical people. I say I'm just obsessed with making sure things make sense and work well. If you're unsure about AI, curious but overwhelmed, or want to test something fast, I'd love to help.

I'm based in the Bay Area, but work remotely with clients everywhere. You can reach out directly via the contact form or book a free call to figure out if AI can solve your particular headache.
